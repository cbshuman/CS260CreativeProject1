Osama Bin Laden Doll

Summary by Jim

Create a doll of their enemy, and give it to families with children. Brilliant! Now the kids get their own terrorist doll to play with! This really happened people, it's on the web from several reputable news organizations.

The plug was reportedly pulled on this project. Likely because the finished faceless doll just looked like Darth Maul being portrayed by Bin Laden, and what little boy doesn't think that Darth Maul looks freaking cool? Just remember that this is what the deep state cooks up with your tax dollars.
